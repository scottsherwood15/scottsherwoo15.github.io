Intro

I am a data analytics professional with experience in business analytics and marketing analytics. Combining my statistics background with my passion for data, I strive to break into the data science world. Check out some of my projects here.

Career

I currently work at Swire Coca-Cola in Draper, UT as a Business Insights Analyst II / Lead Analyst. I have been at Swire Coca-Cola since September of 2021.
Before I joined Swire Coca-Cola, I was a Data Analyst at Bonneville Communications in Salt Lake City, UT analyzing marketing data for a multitude of clients including the State of Utah, Days of '47 Rodeo, and more.

About

I graduated from the University of Utah in May of 2020 with a Bachelor's of Science in Mathematics with my Emphasis in Statistics. Some important course work I completed was Inferential Statistics, Time-Series Analysis, Actuarial Mathematics, and Applied Statistics.
I am always looking for ways to continue my education, and am currently enrolled in a Data Science course utilizing Python with DataCamp.com where I'm building on top of my statistics background and rounding out my skills in predictive modeling.

Outside of work, I enjoy spending time outdoors skiing, hiking, camping, road cycling, and traveling.
